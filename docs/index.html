What does "Licenced Sponsor" mean?

Companies with a UK visa sponsor licence can legally sponsor skilled workers for a work visa. We cross-reference job listings with the official UK Government register to verify sponsorship capability. Note: having a licence does not guarantee they will sponsor your specific role.

Glossary

SC Clearance
Security Check — basic UK security clearance for sensitive roles. Takes 6–8 weeks.
DV Clearance
Developed Vetting — highest level of UK security clearance. Takes 6–9 months.
5yr UK Residency
Requirement to have lived continuously in the UK for 5 years (often for security clearance).
Stale Listing
Job posted more than 14 days ago that may no longer be available.
Licenced Company
Company holds an A-rated Skilled Worker sponsor licence. Does not guarantee sponsorship for this role.
Sponsorship Mentioned
The job listing explicitly states visa sponsorship is available. Always confirm with employer.
